WHO WE ARE

We Drive Your Journey Forward

Access Transport Company was established in Kigali, Rwanda, to address the growing demand for elite, trustworthy, and seamless vehicle rental and transportation systems. We serve corporate logistics, tourism clients, diplomatic missions, and leisure travellers alike. Our philosophy is built on client satisfaction, transparency, and top-tier vehicle care.

OUR JOURNEY

Key Milestones

Phase 1

The Beginning

Founded in Kigali, Rwanda with a small, high-quality fleet and a big vision: to redefine transport reliability and passenger logistics in the region by focusing on strict customer service and punctuality.

Phase 2

Growing Our Fleet

Expanding our capacity to include high-end executive SUVs, rugged 4x4s for country tours, spacious safari vans, pickups, and luxury wedding sedans, catering to diverse local and international demands.

Phase 3

Serving Hundreds

Delivering exceptional logistics support to hundreds of satisfied retail clients, corporate organizations, embassies, and event organizers across Kigali, wider Rwanda, and regional border routes.
