Our Journey So Far

The Beginning
Access Transport Company was founded in Kigali with a small fleet and a big vision. We started serving local businesses with reliable and affordable transport solutions.
Growing Our Fleet
We expanded our fleet to include luxury vehicles, 4x4s, vans and pickups. Our client base grew significantly as word spread about our quality of service and commitment to punctuality.
Serving Hundreds
Today we proudly serve hundreds of businesses and individuals across Rwanda. We continue to invest in our fleet and team to deliver the best transport experience possible.
